Important Rules to Know

24-hour cookie: You earn commission if they buy within 24 hours of clicking your link

90-day cart extension: If they add to cart within 24 hours, you get commission if they buy within 90 days

Whole cart commission: You earn on their entire order, not just the disc golf item they clicked

Bounties: Amazon also pays fixed bounties for Amazon Prime sign-ups, Audible trials, and Amazon Business registrations that can supplement disc golf content
