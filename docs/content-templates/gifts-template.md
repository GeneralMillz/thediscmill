# 10 Best Gifts for Disc Golfers Under $50 (2026 Guide)

Buying gifts for a disc golfer can be tricky, especially if you don't know what discs they already throw. The secret is to buy accessories and quality-of-life gear that *every* player needs but rarely buys for themselves.

## 1. The Disc Retriever (The Gift That Pays For Itself)
Every disc golfer eventually throws a $25 disc into a lake or high up into a tree. A telescopic retriever is the ultimate lifesaver.

- **Top Pick**: InZone Kwik Stik XXL or Dynamic Discs Retriever
- **Why it's a great gift**: It extends up to 18 feet and hooks onto the disc, easily saving hundreds of dollars in lost plastic over a season.
- 👉 [Check Price on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Disc+Golf+Retriever+Pole&tag=thediscmill-20)

## 2. Grip Enhancers (For Sweaty Hands)
When playing in the summer heat, sweaty hands lead to slipped drives and missed putts.

- **Top Pick**: Whale Sacs or Friction Gloves
- **Why it's a great gift**: Whale Sacs are clay-filled bags shaped like whales that absorb moisture. Friction Gloves are perfect for playing in the rain.
- 👉 [Check Price on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Whale+Sacs+Disc+Golf&tag=thediscmill-20)

## 3. UV Flashlight (For Night Golf)
Glow disc golf is incredibly popular during the shorter winter days.

- **Top Pick**: Vansky 51 LED UV Flashlight
- **Why it's a great gift**: A standard flashlight won't charge a glow disc. A UV light charges it instantly.
- 👉 [Check Price on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Vansky+51+LED+UV+Flashlight&tag=thediscmill-20)

## 4. Premium Microfiber Towels
You can never have too many towels on the course to wipe mud and dew off discs.

- **Top Pick**: Flight Towel or Carabiner Microfiber Towels
- **Why it's a great gift**: The Flight Towel includes a piece of a real disc attached to the towel, doubling as a warm-up tool before a round.
- 👉 [Check Price on Amazon](https://www.amazon.com/s?k=Flight+Towel+Disc+Golf&tag=thediscmill-20)

## 5. A Mystery Box
If you really want to buy them discs but don't know what to get, let the manufacturer decide.

- **Top Pick**: Discmania or MVP Mystery Box
- **Why it's a great gift**: It's like opening a pack of baseball cards. They get a mix of putters, midranges, and drivers, often including rare misprints or unreleased prototypes.
