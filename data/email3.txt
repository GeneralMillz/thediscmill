Disc Golf Daily Intelligence Brief — May 10, 2026
1. Daily Summary
Westside Discs dropped two new Originals line molds — the Vellamo midrange (5/5.5/0/1.5) and the DD4 distance driver (13/5/-1/3) — both on May 4th. DD4 is first-ever release and a much-requested addition to the line.
Discmania released the 2026 Cloud Breaker (DD3 retool) in Horizon Q-Line plastic on May 4th alongside Gannon Buhr's Signature Series.
Discraft's 2026 PDGA Worlds fundraiser discs landed at retailers May 1st with limitedcollector runs — including Nate Doss CryZtal Buzzz and Ezra Robinson's "Peach Pirate" Midnight Cigarra.
Axiom/MVP released the Neutron Balance (Simon Line, 5/5/0/2) — Simon Lizotte's latest stock-release midrange joining the Pixel and Time-Lapse.
Lightning Discs retooled the Rubber Putter with improved rubber blends, back in production May 9th.
Ran Disc Golf dropped a Trash Panda retailer-exclusive in recycled orange metal flake plastic — grippy, slightly more stable than stock.
A wave of 2026 Tour Series releases hit retailers this week from Prodigy and Discraft (Ledgestone edition), alongside new Gregg Barsby Duo Roadrunner from Innova.
